About


We are the perfect Marketplace for teens

TeenB is an innovative platform that allows teenangers to learn and earn at the same time. It was initially ideated in 2019 by a group of school-going teenagers in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The purpose behind it was to tackle global scale issues involving the education sector, such as lack of educational opportunities, as well as lack of financial literacy in teenagers.

TeenB is an online marketplace, aimed at teenagers, that allows users and providers to avail and offer services. After the idea was formed, TeenB achieved great success as the project won the Gems Global Innovation Challenge 2020 and was short-listed as finalists for events such as the Gems Xcelerator Program and Expo 2020 Dubai.
